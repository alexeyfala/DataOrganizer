Windows:
1. Open "Command Line" in Windows.
2. Run:
cd C:\folder_with_app
DataOrganizer.exe --console --debug --help

Linux:
1. Open "Terminal" in Linux.
2. Run:
cd /home/folder_with_app
chmod u+x ./DataOrganizer
./DataOrganizer --console --debug --help

macOS (Installed):
1. Open "Terminal" in macOS.
2. Run:
cd /Applications
open -n ./DataOrganizer.app --args --console --debug --help
